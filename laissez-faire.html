Laissez-Faire

Noun

An economic or political policy of minimal government interference in business or personal affairs.

Collocations:

  • Laissez-faire attitude
  • Laissez-faire capitalism
  • Laissez-faire approach
  • Laissez-faire leadership
  • Adopt a laissez-faire policy
  • Embrace laissez-faire principles
  • A government’s laissez-faire stance

Example Sentences:

  • "The government’s laissez-faire approach allowed businesses to grow with minimal regulation."
  • "He had a laissez-faire attitude toward parenting, allowing his children to make their own decisions."
  • "Laissez-faire capitalism promotes free markets without government intervention."
