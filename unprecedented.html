Unprecedented

Adjective

Never having happened or existed before; unparalleled.

Collocations:

  • Unprecedented event
  • Unprecedented level
  • Unprecedented success
  • Unprecedented scale
  • Unprecedented number
  • Unprecedented growth
  • Unprecedented change
  • Unprecedented crisis
  • Unprecedented achievement
  • Unprecedented situation

Example Sentences:

  • "The company reported an unprecedented level of profits this quarter."
  • "The team achieved unprecedented success in the tournament."
  • "The country is facing an unprecedented crisis due to the pandemic."
  • "The unprecedented growth of the tech industry has transformed the economy."
  • "The unprecedented number of applicants overwhelmed the admissions office."
