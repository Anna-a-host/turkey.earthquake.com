Relentless

Adjective

Continuing without becoming weaker, less severe, or less intense. Often used to describe something persistent, unyielding, or harsh.

Collocations:

  • Relentless pursuit
  • Relentless pressure
  • Relentless determination
  • Relentless effort
  • Relentless force

Example Sentences:

  • "The team showed relentless determination to win the championship."
  • "His relentless pursuit of excellence inspired others."
