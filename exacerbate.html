Exacerbate

Verb

To make a bad situation, problem, or feeling worse.

Collocations:

  • Exacerbate the problem
  • Exacerbate tensions
  • Exacerbate the situation
  • Exacerbate the conflict
  • Exacerbate the crisis
  • Exacerbate the issue
  • Exacerbate the condition
  • Exacerbate the symptoms
  • Exacerbate the pain
  • Exacerbate the difficulties

Example Sentences:

  • "The new law only exacerbates the problem."
  • "The attack will exacerbate the already tense relations between the two communities."
  • "The proposed factory shutdown would only exacerbate our unemployment problems."
  • "His angry comments have exacerbated tensions in the negotiation process."
  • "The declining retirement security faced by growing numbers of Americans is being exacerbated by increasing longevity and quickly rising health care costs."
