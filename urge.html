Urge

Verb

To strongly advise or try to persuade someone to do something.

Collocations:

  • Urge someone to do something
  • Strongly urge
  • Urge caution
  • Urge immediate action
  • Urge restraint
  • Repeatedly urge
  • Urgently urge

Example Sentences:

  • "Doctors urge people to quit smoking for better health."
  • "She urged him to reconsider his decision."
  • "The government urged immediate action on climate change."
Noun

A strong desire or impulse to do something.

Collocations:

  • Resist the urge
  • Feel an urge
  • Sudden urge
  • Overwhelming urge
  • Uncontrollable urge
  • Fight the urge

Example Sentences:

  • "He felt a sudden urge to call her."
  • "I resisted the urge to eat another cookie."
  • "She had an overwhelming urge to scream."
