Counsel

Noun

Advice, guidance, or support, especially given by a professional or trusted individual.

Collocations:

  • To seek counsel
  • To offer counsel
  • Legal counsel
  • Counsel for [someone]
  • To give counsel

Example Sentences:

  • "She sought counsel from a financial advisor before making the investment."
  • "The lawyer offered counsel to the defendant throughout the trial."
  • "He was happy to offer counsel to his younger colleagues."
Verb

To give advice or guidance, especially to someone who is in need of help or support.

Collocations:

  • To counsel [someone] on [something]
  • To counsel against [something]
  • To counsel on [a decision]
  • To counsel someone through [a difficult situation]

Example Sentences:

  • "The doctor counseled him on how to manage his stress levels."
  • "She counseled the student against dropping out of school."
  • "The counselor helped her through the difficult period of grief."
