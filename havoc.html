Havoc

Noun

A situation in which there is a lot of destruction, confusion, or disorder.

Collocations:

  • Wreak havoc
  • Cause havoc
  • Create havoc
  • Spread havoc
  • Total havoc
  • Economic havoc
  • Chaos and havoc

Example Sentences:

  • "The hurricane caused havoc across the entire region."
  • "The power outage wreaked havoc on the transportation system."
  • "Misinformation on social media can spread havoc quickly."
