Mount

Noun

1. A mountain.

2. A support or structure on which something is placed or fixed.

Example: "They reached the summit of Mount Everest after a challenging climb."

Verb

1. To go up or ascend.

2. To increase or rise.

3. To organize or prepare.

4. To place on a support or backing.

Example: "She mounted the podium to receive her award."

  • Mount an exhibition
  • Mount a protest
  • Mount a defense
  • Mount a challenge
  • Mount a horse
