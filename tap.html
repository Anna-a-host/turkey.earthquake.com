Tap into

Phrasal Verb

To make use of something, usually a resource or potential, in order to achieve a specific result or gain access to something valuable.

Collocations:

  • To tap into potential
  • To tap into resources
  • To tap into a market
  • To tap into knowledge
  • To tap into emotions
  • To tap into creativity

Example Sentences:

  • "The company is looking to tap into new markets in Asia."
  • "She was able to tap into her creative side to solve the problem."
  • "They tapped into their emotional strengths to overcome the challenge."
