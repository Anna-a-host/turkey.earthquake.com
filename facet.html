Facet

Noun

One part or aspect of something, especially a problem, situation, or subject.

Collocations:

  • A facet of life
  • A facet of personality
  • A crucial facet
  • An important facet
  • The many facets of [something]
  • Different facets of [something]
  • Examine all facets
  • Explore every facet

Example Sentences:

  • "Honesty is an important facet of a strong relationship."
  • "The book explores different facets of human nature."
  • "We need to examine all facets of the issue before making a decision."
