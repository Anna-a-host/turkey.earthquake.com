Tailor

Verb

To make or adapt something for a particular purpose or person.

Collocations:

  • To tailor something for [someone]
  • To tailor a product/service
  • To tailor a solution
  • To tailor a message
  • To tailor the approach

Example Sentences:

  • "The company tailored its services to meet the specific needs of each customer."
  • "He tailored his approach to fit the unique challenges of the project."
  • "The marketing team tailored the campaign to target a younger audience."
  • "She tailored her presentation to suit the interests of the audience."
