Compel

Verb

To force someone to do something or make something happen through pressure or necessity.

Collocations:

  • Compel someone to do something
  • Compel obedience
  • Feel compelled to act
  • Compel attention
  • Compel respect
  • Be compelled by circumstances
  • Compel compliance

Example Sentences:

  • "The law compels companies to provide accurate information to consumers."
  • "She felt compelled to speak out against injustice."
  • "His powerful speech compelled everyone in the room to listen."
