Compulsive

Adjective

Having an irresistible urge to do something repeatedly, often leading to behavior that is difficult to control.

Collocations:

  • Compulsive behavior
  • Compulsive disorder
  • Compulsive need
  • Compulsive liar
  • Compulsive eating
  • Compulsive gambler

Example Sentences:

  • "His compulsive behavior made it hard for him to focus on anything else."
  • "She struggled with compulsive eating habits that affected her health."
  • "The compulsive need to check his phone was distracting during meetings."
  • "The therapist diagnosed him with a compulsive disorder and recommended treatment."
