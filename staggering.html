Staggering

Adjective

Extremely surprising, shocking, or difficult to believe due to its size, amount, or effect.

Collocations:

  • A staggering amount
  • A staggering figure
  • A staggering sum
  • A staggering achievement
  • A staggering cost
  • A staggering success
  • A staggering speed
  • A staggering number
  • Absolutely staggering
  • Quite staggering

Example Sentences:

  • "The company reported a staggering loss of $10 billion."
  • "The speed at which technology is advancing is staggering."
  • "She made a staggering recovery after the accident."
