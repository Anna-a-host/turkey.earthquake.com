Fleet

Noun

1. A group of vehicles, ships, or aircraft operated under unified control.

2. A number of warships under a single command.

Example: "The company expanded its fleet to include electric vehicles."

Adjective

1. Swift or fast in movement.

Example: "The fleet runner won the race effortlessly."

Verb

1. To move swiftly or pass by quickly.

Example: "Time seemed to fleet as they enjoyed their vacation."

Common Collocations:

  • Fleet of vehicles
  • Naval fleet
  • Fleet management
  • Fleet admiral
  • Fleet-footed
