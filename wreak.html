Wreak

Verb

To cause a lot of damage, harm, or suffering.

Collocations:

  • Wreak havoc
  • Wreak destruction
  • Wreak revenge
  • Wreak chaos
  • Wreak devastation
  • Wreak disaster

Example Sentences:

  • "The storm wreaked havoc on the coastal towns."
  • "His reckless behavior wreaked chaos in the office."
